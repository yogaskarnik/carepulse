# CarePulse - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

### Name
**CarePulse**

### Slogan
**"Connected to care."**

### One-liner
An AI-powered safety network that detects risks early, alerts caregivers, and responds fast in emergencies for vulnerable adults.

### Brand Icon
💙 (Blue Heart) - Represents compassion, care, and health monitoring

### Brand Colors
- **Primary**: Blue (#3B82F6) - Trust, healthcare, technology
- **Success**: Green (#10B981) - Safety, health, positive outcomes
- **Warning**: Amber (#F59E0B) - Caution, attention needed
- **Danger**: Red (#EF4444) - Critical, emergency, urgent action

## Key Messages

### Core Value Proposition
CarePulse connects caregivers to their loved ones through intelligent monitoring. We detect risks before they become emergencies and respond fast when it matters most.

### Key Differentiators
1. **Early Detection** - AI learns patterns and catches anomalies before incidents occur
2. **Fast Response** - Automatic alerts and emergency network priority (QoS)
3. **Network-Level Intelligence** - Uses telecom APIs for reliable, always-on monitoring
4. **Peace of Mind** - Reduces caregiver anxiety with 24/7 oversight

### Target Audience
- **Primary**: Family caregivers of elderly parents/relatives
- **Secondary**: Professional care facilities and home healthcare providers
- **Tertiary**: Insurance companies seeking risk reduction

## Messaging Framework

### Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
"CarePulse is an AI-powered safety network for elderly care. We use telecom network intelligence and machine learning to detect risks early - like unusual locations, lack of movement, or fraud attempts. When something's wrong, we alert caregivers instantly and even activate emergency network priority. From 6 hours to find grandma, to 30 seconds."

### Problem Statement
1.4 billion elderly people worldwide face daily safety risks: getting lost, falls, medical emergencies, and fraud. Caregivers live with constant fear and stress, lacking visibility until it's too late.

### Solution Statement
CarePulse provides 24/7 monitoring that learns each person's patterns and detects anomalies automatically. We alert caregivers before incidents escalate and ensure emergency calls get priority network access.

### Impact Statement
We're building a safety net that lets elderly people maintain independence while giving families peace of mind. Every second counts in emergencies - we make sure help arrives fast.

## Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- **Professional** - We handle serious, life-critical situations
- **Compassionate** - We understand the emotional weight of caregiving
- **Clear** - No jargon, straightforward communication
- **Reassuring** - Calm confidence, not alarmist

### Tone Guidelines
- **DO**: Use warm, supportive language that acknowledges caregiver challenges
- **DO**: Be direct about risks and actions needed
- **DO**: Emphasize prevention and early detection
- **DON'T**: Use fear-based marketing or guilt
- **DON'T**: Over-promise or minimize real challenges
- **DON'T**: Use overly technical language without explanation

## Usage Examples

### Marketing Copy
❌ **Bad**: "Protect your loved ones with cutting-edge AI algorithms and 5G network slicing"
✅ **Good**: "Stay connected to the people you care about. CarePulse watches over them, so you don't have to worry."

### Alert Messages
❌ **Bad**: "CRITICAL ANOMALY DETECTED IN GEOFENCE PARAMETERS"
✅ **Good**: "Maria left her safe zone. Time to check in and make sure she's okay."

### Feature Descriptions
❌ **Bad**: "Leverages ML-powered behavioral clustering for anomaly classification"
✅ **Good**: "Learns daily routines and notices when something unusual happens"

## Product Naming Conventions

### Features
- **Safe Zones** (not "geofences")
- **Risk Score** (not "anomaly score")
- **Emergency Priority** (not "QoS activation")
- **Daily Patterns** (not "behavioral models")

### Actions
- **Check In** (not "verify")
- **Update Now** (not "refresh")
- **Add Patient** (not "create patient record")

## Visual Identity

### Logo
Blue heart icon (💙) + "CarePulse" wordmark in Inter font

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (professional, readable, modern)
- **Headings**: Inter Bold/ExtraBold
- **Body**: Inter Regular/Medium
- **Monospace**: (for technical data like coordinates)

### UI Elements
- **Rounded corners**: 6-8px (friendly, approachable)
- **Shadows**: Subtle, soft (professional depth)
- **Spacing**: Clean, generous whitespace
- **Cards**: Clean borders, subtle shadows
- **Buttons**: Clear CTAs with adequate padding

### Iconography
- Use emoji sparingly but strategically
- 📍 Location
- 🏠 Home/Safe Zone
- 💡 Recommendations
- 🔔 Alerts
- 📱 Device
- 🔋 Battery
