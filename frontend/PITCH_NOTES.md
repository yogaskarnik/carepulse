## 🎤 Delivery Tips

### Voice & Body Language:
- **Speak 20% slower than normal** - You're nervous, judges are tired
- **Pause after key stats** - Let "8 seconds" sink in
- **Make eye contact** - Scan all 5 judges, not just one
- **Stand still** - Don't pace, don't fidget
- **Use hand gestures sparingly** - Point to screen for demo only

### Pronunciation:
- "au-TON-o-mous-ly" (practice this)
- "Model Context Protocol" (say slowly)
- "GEO-fen-cing" (not "geo-fencing")

### Reference Scoring Criteria:
Explicitly say these phrases:
- "This demonstrates **purposeful innovation**..."
- "Our **business justification** shows immediate ROI..."
- "This solves a **real, near-term problem**..."
- "The **business relevance** is clear..."

### Demo Narration Rules:
1. **Say what you're doing BEFORE you click**
2. **Narrate what the AI is doing as it happens**
3. **Point to specific parts of screen** ("See here, the risk score...")
4. **Pause to let judges process** (3 seconds after AI completes)
5. **Connect back to business value** ("This entire workflow took 8 seconds...")

## 💡 Anticipated Questions & Answers

**Q: "What if the patient doesn't have a 5G phone?"**
A: "Works with 4G as well. 5G gives us faster response and better accuracy, but system works on any connected device with Nokia's network."

**Q: "How do you prevent false alarms?"**
A: "Gemini AI analyzes multiple data points - not just one signal. Battery low + outside safe zone + unusual pattern = alert. Single anomaly = monitored but no alert. Plus, caregivers can adjust sensitivity thresholds."

**Q: "What about privacy concerns?"**
A: "Location data encrypted end-to-end. Only caregiver has access. GDPR compliant. Patient can disable tracking anytime. Data stored in EU only."

**Q: "How is this different from Apple Watch fall detection?"**
A: "Apple Watch detects falls after they happen. CarePulse predicts risk BEFORE emergencies through pattern analysis, geofencing, and network monitoring. Plus, our autonomous AI activates emergency network priority - Apple can't do that."

**Q: "What's your business model?"**
A: "B2C: €35/month subscription for families. B2B: Licensing to care facilities and insurance companies. Revenue share with Nokia on API usage."

**Q: "Can this scale?"**
A: "Yes. Built on Nokia's global network infrastructure. One Gemini AI agent can monitor 1000+ patients concurrently. Cloud-deployed for infinite scale."

**Q: "Why MCP instead of direct API calls?"**
A: "MCP enables true agentic behavior. The AI decides which tools to use and when - it's not following hardcoded rules. This makes the system adaptive and intelligent, not just automated."
